Factoring & Solving

Quadratic: Use the quadratic formula x = (-b +/- sqrt(b^2 - 4ac)) / (2a) when factoring fails.
Rational: Find common denominators and always verify solutions to eliminate extraneous values.
Radical: Isolate the radical, square carefully, then check solutions in the original equation.

Logarithm Rulebook

Logs are exponents in another form.

log(ab) = log(a) + log(b)
log(a/b) = log(a) - log(b)
log(a^n) = n * log(a)
log_b(x) = log_c(x) / log_c(b)
